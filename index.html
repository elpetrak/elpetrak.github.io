About Me

My name is Eleni Petraki. I am a PhD candidate at the Database Architectures group of the Dutch National Research Center for Mathematics and Computer Science (CWI) in Amsterdam, The Netherlands. I work with Prof. Martin Kersten (Univerity of Amsterdam), Prof. Stefan Manegold (University of Leiden) and Asst. Prof. Stratos Idreos (from Harvard University). My PhD focuses on automatic physical design in main-memory column-oriented database management systems. My research interests include database architectures, adaptive indexing, multidimensional indexing and parallel query processing.
